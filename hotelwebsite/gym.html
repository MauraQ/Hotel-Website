Maya Escencia Gym

We make it easy to maintain your fitness routine while soaking in the beauty of the Tulum. Our fully equipped fitness center combines modern exercise equipment with inspiring ocean views, allowing you to work out in comfort and style.

What's included?

  • Cardio equipment: treadmills, stationary bikes, ellipticals, and rowing machines
  • Strength training: free weights, resistance machines, and functional training zones
  • Dedicated space for yoga, stretching, or Pilates sessions
  • Refreshing water stations and complimentary towels
  • Personal training and wellness guidance (available on request)

For guests who prefer outdoor exercise, we also offer options like guided beach workouts, sunrise yoga on the deck, or jogging paths along the shoreline.
