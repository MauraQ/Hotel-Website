Maya Escencia Resturant & Bar

At the heart of every dish we serve is a story — one that began thousands of years ago, deep in the jungles and villages of the Yucatán Peninsula. Our kitchen honors the culinary traditions of the ancient Maya, whose respect for the land, the sea, and the cycles of nature shaped a cuisine both simple and sacred. Each meal at our table is more than food — it’s an offering to the ancestors, a celebration of life’s cycles, and a reminder that authenticity lives in the details: the scent of roasting chiles, the warmth of a handmade tortilla, the taste of the wind and earth in every bite.

Come enjoy one of our refresing Margaritas!

The bar and resturant is open everyday from 8am-2am. Reservations are not required but highly recommended during weekends. Beach front seating is available upon request!
